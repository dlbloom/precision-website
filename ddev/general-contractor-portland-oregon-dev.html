CONSTRUCTION SERVICES
General Contractors, Commercial Construction, Church Construction
Precision Construction, located in Portland, Oregon, specializes in general construction, church construction, retail construction, and public and private commercial construction.  Our expertise includes brand new facilities, renovations, multi-story tenant improvements, retail strip malls, restaurants, luxury residential construction, and computer clean-room work.  Take a look below to find out more detailed information about what we’re capable of and how we are the right fit for your project.

 
   
 
COMMERCIAL-PUBLIC
The most recent example of Precision Construction’s excellence in commercial public work is the Tillamook Forest Interpretive Center. Complete with a 16,000 square foot building housing a forestry museum, classrooms, and theater in the heart of the Tillamook forest, the Interpretive Center also includes a breathtaking walking bridge over the Wilson River and a vintage replica Forest Fire Lookout Tower.  But most impressively, we were able to move one of Oregon’s biggest cranes into this pristine forest and back out again without leaving one lasting mark. COMMERCIAL-PRIVATE
With clients such as Erickson Realty, Harsch Investments, Henry V Events, Verizon Communications, Delta Airlines, and Intel, Precision Construction’s private commercial projects have ranged from clean-room facilities to full floor remodels to new facility construction.  In addition to luxury finishing work, staircases, and executive office and lobby construction, Precision also performs extensive electrical and mechanical upgrades, HVAC systems, and new building additions.
 
CHURCH CONSTRUCTION
Precision Construction has a proud history of partnering with area churches to help them achieve their construction goals, often delivering projects ahead of schedule and under budget.  In the case of Abundant Life Christian Church in Boring, Oregon, Precision Construction constructed a new 35,000 square foot multi-purpose sanctuary involving steel construction and panelized roof.  Through excellent control of resources, timetables, and management, we were able to save the church enough money to fund the next phase of their development; a brand new fellowship hall.
 
RETAIL
Whether you need a brand new retail facility, or need to completely renovate an existing space, Precision Construction has the expertise to fit your needs.  Wood frame, steel construction, site development, HVAC systems, building additions, and any other retail construction needs are all handled in an extremely efficient and timely manner to keep your project on time and on budget.  
TENANT IMPROVEMENT
Some of our most rewarding and successful projects have been tenant improvement for such companies as Verizon Communications, Columbia Financial and Erickson Realty.  From full, multi-floor demolition and remodeling to luxury finishing work, Precision Construction has overseen a wide variety of project sizes and scopes, managing and coordinating all aspects of the job from demolition to new mechanical and electrical system replacement.
INDUSTRIAL CONSTRUCTION
From chemical processing facilities to state-of-the-art blood testing facilities, Precision Construction has the skill and expertise for industrial construction of the highest standards.  Based on a successful Phase I development, Precision was selected by the National Red Cross to complete the second phase of their Regional Blood Testing Facility, a project which required extensive knowledge of current testing requirements, atmosphere maintenance, and mechanical systems.
RESIDENTIAL
When it comes to unsurpassed luxury in residential construction, Precision Construction brings the art, expertise, and experience needed to your dream home. Excavation, basement additions, structural framing, millwork, site development, suspended exterior concrete and wood decking – are all executed with exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail, bringing fine art and luxury construction home to you.
