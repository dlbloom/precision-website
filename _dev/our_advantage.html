OUR ADVANTAGE
Precision Construction is a subsidiary of Hoffman Construction, one of the largest general contractors in the United States. Because of this relationship, we have access to an impressive array of equipment, tools, facilities, experienced personnel, and one of the strongest reputations in general contracting in the nation.
 

CONSTRUCTION ADVANTAGE
Precision Construction has an experienced staff with expertise in every facet of the construction field, from carpentry to management.  And because of our relationship with Hoffman Construction, we have access to the Northwest’s premier sub-contractors and construction vendors. We follow proven value-engineering plans that can identify cost savings early in the construction process, and encourage our subcontractors to actively participate in constructability reviews to ensure projects stay on time and on budget.  From large scale, high-end residential structures to computer clean-room facilities to restaurants and churches, the depth and breadth of our experience ensures that we have the right skills, abilities, and know-how for your project.

 

CONDOMINIUM ADVANTAGE
As a subsidiary of Hoffman Construction, Precision Construction has a priceless knowledge of luxury high-rise condominium construction.  We have experience and expertise in the aspects most critical to condominium construction, including mechanical systems, fire sprinkler systems, security systems, and electrical panels.  We also have the capabilities and know-how to upgrade, remodel, renovate, and maintain luxury units to be the condominium of your dreams – from the planning and design to the construction and maintenance of new fireplace mantels, bookshelves, closet systems, new kitchens and bathrooms, and much more.
